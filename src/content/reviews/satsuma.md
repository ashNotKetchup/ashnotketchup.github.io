This might have been the easiest book I've ever read—straightforward, good pacing, suitably mortim-esque. Don't expect it to change your life, but do expect a good week or two absorbed in the surreal world of Gary Thorn's South London.

It was thoroughly entertaining, but equally forgettable. Better than Instagram on the tube.

(3 Satsumas)
